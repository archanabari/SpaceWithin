Meet Archana Datir

Certified Counselor & Life Coach

Archana believes that true change happens when we create *space within* to hear our own voice. With years of experience in personal development and counseling, her approach is rooted in compassion, practicality, and empowerment. She works closely with individuals to identify deep-seated patterns, foster self-awareness, and develop actionable strategies to navigate life's complexities successfully.

Whether you are seeking clarity in your career, managing a major life transition, or simply aiming for better emotional health, Archana is here to guide you with a non-judgmental and supportive presence.
